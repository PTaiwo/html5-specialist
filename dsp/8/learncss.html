HTML5 Specialist Program

The HTML5 Specialist Program is designed to provide developers with proof of their coding skills. The program includes a course and an exam, upon completion of this course and exam you will receive the HTML5 Specialist Designation.

Upon earning the HTML5 Specialist Designation, you can display the HTML5 Specialist badge in your resume, portfolio and your LinkedIn profile. You will also receive a digital certificate you can print and frame.
